# Hi, I'm Maja Obrzut 👋
**Physiotherapist | Professional Ski Instructor (Livigno, Italy) | Sports & Relax Massage Therapist (Elbląg, Poland)**

Welcome to my digital profile! I am a qualified physiotherapist who bridges the gap between sports biomechanics, injury prevention, and deep body regeneration.

My professional life is divided into two seasonal passions, both rooted in my medical and physiotherapeutic background:

## ❄️ Winter: Alpine Skiing in Livigno, Italy
During the winter season, I run **MajaSKI - Akademia Sportów Śnieżnych** (Snow Sports Academy) in Livigno.
* **Credentials:** POL-SKI Professional Instructor **ISIA** certification and the Italian **Maestro di Sci** title. Instructing since **2011**.
* **Specialization:** I teach downhill skiing, snowboarding, and telemark to both children and adults.
* **Approach:** As a physiotherapist, my teaching focuses heavily on the biomechanics of movement and joint safety on the slopes.
* **Website:** [MajaSKI - Polska Szkoła Narciarska Livigno](https://majaski.pl)

## ☀️ Summer & Off-season: Physiotherapy & Massage in Elbląg, Poland
When the snow melts, I return to physiotherapy and massage in **Elbląg** — **mobile sessions** at your home or preferred location.
* **Experience:** I gained my experience working in several European SPAs.
* **Specialization:** Custom full-body relaxation massages, signature lifting facial massages (a natural alternative to aesthetic medicine), hot-stone rituals, and lymphatic leg compression — ideal for post-ski recovery.
* **Website:** [Maja Masuje - Masaż i Fizjoterapia Elbląg](https://majamasuje.pl)
